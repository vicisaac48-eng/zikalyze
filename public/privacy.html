1. Introduction

Welcome to Zikalyze ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy through a client-side architecture with end-to-end encryption. This Privacy Policy explains how our AI-powered cryptocurrency analysis platform (the "Service") operates without collecting your personal data.

Key Principle: Zikalyze is designed with privacy at its core. All your data remains on your device, encrypted with military-grade encryption. We have zero access to your personal information.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

Unlike most applications, Zikalyze does NOT collect the following:

  • NO Personal Information: No names, email addresses, or contact details
  • NO Wallet Data: Your wallet private keys never leave your device
  • NO Passwords: Passwords are used only locally for encryption
  • NO Trading Activity: We don't track your portfolio or trades
  • NO Browsing History: We don't monitor what you do in the app
  • NO Location Data: We don't track where you are
  • NO Analytics Data: No usage tracking sent to our servers
  • NO Cookies for Tracking: Only essential cookies for app functionality
Simple Truth: If we don't collect it, we can't lose it, sell it, or be forced to hand it over.

3. What IS Stored on Your Device (Client-Side Only)

The following data is stored ONLY on your device using encrypted browser storage:

3.1 Encrypted Wallet Data

  • Private Keys: Encrypted with AES-256-GCM using your password
  • Wallet Addresses: Derived from your encrypted keys
  • Username: Stored locally for wallet generation

3.2 App Preferences

  • Cryptocurrency watchlist (which coins you track)
  • Price alert settings
  • Notification preferences
  • Display settings (theme, language)

3.3 Authentication State

  • Session tokens (encrypted, device-only)
  • Login status

Important: All of this data is encrypted and stored ONLY in your browser's local storage. It never leaves your device. If you clear your browser data, this information is permanently deleted.

4. End-to-End Encryption - How Your Data is Protected

We use military-grade encryption to protect your data on your device:

4.1 Encryption Standards

  • AES-256-GCM: Advanced Encryption Standard with 256-bit keys and Galois/Counter Mode
  • PBKDF2: Password-Based Key Derivation Function 2 with 100,000 iterations
  • Secure Random IVs: Cryptographically secure initialization vectors
  • HMAC Authentication: Ensures data integrity and authenticity

4.2 Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Our zero-knowledge architecture means:

  • Your password never leaves your device
  • All encryption/decryption happens client-side
  • We cannot decrypt your data (we don't have your password)
  • Your private keys are encrypted before being stored locally
  • No plain text sensitive data is ever stored
What This Means: Even if someone gained access to our servers (we don't store your data anyway) or your device storage, they would only see encrypted data that's mathematically impossible to decrypt without your password.

5. How Zikalyze Works (Client-Side Processing)

Understanding how our app works helps you see why your privacy is protected:

  1. You Create a Wallet: Your browser generates a cryptographic wallet from your username and password using PBKDF2 (100,000 iterations). This happens entirely on your device.
  2. Encryption: Your private key is immediately encrypted with AES-256-GCM using your password and stored in your browser's local storage.
  3. Market Data: The app fetches public cryptocurrency prices and blockchain data from third-party APIs (CoinGecko, blockchain explorers). This data is public and not linked to you.
  4. Analysis: Our AI algorithms run entirely in your browser, analyzing the public market data to provide you with insights.
  5. Your Actions: All your interactions, preferences, and portfolio data stay on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers.

Result: You get powerful crypto analysis while maintaining complete privacy.

6. Third-Party Services We Use (Public Data Only)

While we don't collect your data, we do use third-party services to provide you with public market information:

6.1 Cryptocurrency Market Data

  • CoinGecko API: Public cryptocurrency prices, market caps, and volume data
  • No personal data transmitted - only requests for public coin prices

6.2 Blockchain Data

  • Blockchain Explorers: Public blockchain transaction data (Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.)
  • Whale Tracking: Public large transaction data from blockchain APIs
  • No personal data transmitted - only requests for public blockchain data

6.3 Optional: Push Notifications

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): If you choose to enable push notifications
  • What's collected: Device token (to send you notifications), notification preferences
  • No personal data: The device token is anonymous and not linked to your identity
  • You control this: Notifications are optional and can be disabled anytime

Important: These third-party services receive NO personal information from you through Zikalyze. They only receive requests for public data that anyone can access.

7. Your Data, Your Control

Since all your data is stored only on your device, you have complete control:

7.1 Access Your Data

All your data is in your browser's local storage. You can access it anytime through your browser's developer tools (though it will be encrypted).

7.2 Delete Your Data

You can delete all your data instantly:

Since we don't store your data, there's nothing for us to export. Your data is already on your device. You can back up your encrypted wallet credentials if you wish.

7.4 Port Your Data

Your wallet is deterministically generated from your username and password. You can recreate it on any device by entering the same credentials.
